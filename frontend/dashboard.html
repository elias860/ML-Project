About the System

The Student Risk Prediction System has been developed to address the critical need for early identification of students who may be at risk academically. By analyzing multiple factors including health status, attendance records, scholarship standing, and academic performance (GPA), this system provides educational institutions with a powerful tool to proactively support students before they face serious academic challenges. The system employs advanced machine learning techniques to process student data and generate comprehensive risk assessments, enabling targeted interventions and support strategies.

How It Works

The system operates through a sophisticated two-step process. Initially, it processes uploaded student data through a machine learning model that analyzes various student metrics including health conditions, attendance patterns, scholarship status, and academic performance indicators. After processing, the system generates detailed visualizations that provide comprehensive insights into student risk levels. These visualizations include distribution analyses, correlation studies between different factors, and trend analyses, allowing educators to make data-driven decisions in supporting student success. The interactive dashboard enables users to upload data, view predictions, and analyze various aspects of student performance through multiple analytical charts.
